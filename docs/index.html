Likes

What Pepper loves more than anything else comes down to one thing: sticks. If you take Pepper on a walk, she's gotta have a stick. You can try to use toys, treats, and your own charisma to get Pepper to come to you, but those who truly know Pepper can easily lure her in with a simple stick. Pepper is a fan of the simple things in life. She also likes the snow, doing big stretches, and sticking her head out of the car window.

Dislikes

Some of Pepper's least favorite things include packing and not being outside. If she sees you packing for a trip, you'll likely get a solemn stare and disappointed head nod as she leaves to to pack. She's not mad, just disappointed. As for going outside, Pepper is persistent. She will patiently wait by the door, maybe throwing in some adorable pawing at it, until she is happily running around her backyard, or sitting in her favorite spot on the deck
